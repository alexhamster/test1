Text: Product Description The Conservation Rooflight is the original low profile skylight, combining the highest modern performance standards with an authentic traditional appearance. Favoured by English Heritage, the National Trust and Planning / Conservation Officers, it is available in a wide range of sizes, along with a Bespoke Design Service.
