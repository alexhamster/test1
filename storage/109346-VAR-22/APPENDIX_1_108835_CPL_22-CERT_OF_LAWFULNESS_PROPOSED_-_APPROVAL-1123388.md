Text: CERTIFICATE OF LAWFUL PROPOSED USE OR DEVELOPMENT The Trafford Borough Council hereby certify that on 4th August 2022 the use described in the First Schedule to this certificate in respect of the land specified in the Second Schedule to this certificate and edged red on the plan attached to this certificate, would have been lawful within the meaning of section 192 of the Town and Country Planning Act 1990 (as amended), for the following reason(s):

Text: CERTIFICATE OF LAWFUL PROPOSED USE OR DEVELOPMENT The existing lawful restaurant use of Unit 3C, 5A and 5B, as shown on the submitted plan, drawing number 11509-001, Issue 1, received by the local planning authority 23 September 2022, and the proposed use of the same unit as a 'massage centre' in accordance with the details set out in the covering letter from White Peak Planning dated 3rd August 2022, both fall under Class E, Part A, Schedule 2 of the Town and Country Planning (Use Classes) (Amendment) (England) Regulations 2020. Therefore the proposed use of the unit as a massage centre, in accordance with the details set out in the covering letter from White Peak Planning dated 3rd August 2022, would not constitute a material change of use as defined by Section 55 of the Town and Country Planning Act 1990 and would be lawful development, subject to compliance with Condition 2 attached to the original grant of planning permission for the restaurant use (H/63231), which states that the premises shall not be open to customers outside the following hours: 1100 hours to midnight Mondays to Sundays.
