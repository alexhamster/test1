Text: 21 Living Ltd The following specification has been developed in accordance with recommendations from ADC Acoustics to achieve the ratings as required by Part E of the Building Regulations:

Text: FLOOR BETWEEN RETAIL & APARTMENT 1 FLOOR BETWEEN RETAIL & APARTMENT 1

Text: FLOOR BETWEEN RETAIL & APARTMENT 1 The existing floor will be upgrade from above with the existing ceiling remaining in place. The floorboards will be lifted and replaced.

Text: FLOOR BETWEEN RETAIL & APARTMENT 1 “Hush Slab 100” 100mm mineral wool insulation installed between joists, supported by “Hush Mesh FireWire” providing 60mins fire insulation and 45 DnTw+CtrdB reduction in airborne sound.

Text: FLOOR BETWEEN APARTMENT 1 & APARTMENT 2 FLOOR BETWEEN APARTMENT 1 & APARTMENT 2

Text: FLOOR BETWEEN APARTMENT 1 & APARTMENT 2 The existing floor will be upgraded from above with the existing ceiling remaining in place.

Text: FLOOR BETWEEN APARTMENT 1 & APARTMENT 2 “Hush Slab 100” 100mm mineral wool insulation installed between joists, supported by “Hush Mesh FireWire” providing 60mins fire insulation and 45 DnTw+CtrdB reduction in airborne sound.

Text: FLOOR BETWEEN APARTMENT 1 & APARTMENT 2 Additionally, Hush Panel 28 floating floor, between partitions, will be installed to provide impact sound rating of no more than 62 L’nTwdB.

Text: FLANKING TRANSMISSION FLANKING TRANSMISSION

Text: FLANKING TRANSMISSION Existing solid walls are to be taken back to brickwork with 15mm plasterboard to be installed on timber battens to reduce flanking transmission through the structure.

Text: WALL SEPARATING STAIRWELL FROM APARTMENTS WALL SEPARATING STAIRWELL FROM APARTMENTS

Text: WALL SEPARATING STAIRWELL FROM APARTMENTS A new Gypwall Staggared partition ref. A233022 (EN) will be constructed, consisting of two layers of Gyproc SoundBloc 12.5mm each side of Gypframe 60 I 70 ‘I’ Studs alternating at 300mm centres, with 50mm Isover Acoustic Partition Roll (APR 1200) in the cavity achieving 48 DnTw+CtrdB.
