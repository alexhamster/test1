If you need to view a paper copy of forms and plan(s) you will need to make an appointment to do so and the plans will be made available for viewing at Access Trafford, Waterside House, Sale Waterside, Sale M33 7ZF during opening hours, viz. Monday to Friday 9.00 am to 5.00 pm and Saturday 10.00 am to 4.00 pm. Please ring 0161 912 3149 in order to make an appointment giving at least 24 hours notice to allow time for the documents to be made available.
