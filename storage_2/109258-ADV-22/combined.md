Text: Heritage Statement Unit 10a is situated on the first floor of a 3-storey building. The unit located on the first floor sits within the Goose Green Conservation area and is accessed through a ground floor entrance located on the perimeter of the Green.

Text: Heritage Statement Goose Green is described as an historic hamlet, which currently contains a selection of modest buildings which boast a diverse selection of restaurants, bars, retail and other amenities. It can be located slightly south-east towards the towns centre, within the borough of Trafford.

Text: Heritage Statement Unit 10a sits within a Three storey Victorian/Georgian building and overlooks the Goose Green area. Although the building itself is not listed, it does possess a high level of character and is a positive component of the conservation area. The consistent use of brick and domestic design is paired with the a diverse mix of appropriate shop fronts. The building stands around an historic open space adding to the prestige of the Goose Green Conservation Area.
